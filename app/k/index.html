Publish kits

A publish kit is what a producer needs to take a meeting out to the world: the clips worth cutting, and draft copy to ship them with — titles, a description, chapters — all assembled from the transcript, no model. Every kit here reads in the browser; cutting the video and burning captions happen in Publisher at the desk, and each kit downloads as a file that opens there.
